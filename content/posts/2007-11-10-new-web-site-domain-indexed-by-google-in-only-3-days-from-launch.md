_**Overview:**_

  * Call from a friend about need for a web site to publish a story
  * Receipt of eleven (11) page Word doc.
  * Summary of objective : Publish the story as collected over the last year by investigators to inform and alert contributors to a charity with regard to ongoing investigation and allegations of abuse.
  * Execution of plan by using a new installation of WordPress with four (4) pages & twenty-four (24) posts derived from the Word doc.
  * Target blogs to get traffic: Posting on Blogger, a friends blog, and mySpace
_**Client History:**_

  * Previous site, Miracle-ranch.org recently taken down, now offline.
  * 9/27/07-9/29/07 : bought up target domains
_**Timeline begins 10/18/07:**_

  * Friend calls and emails me on 10/18/07 to share his story
  * Goal of ranking next or above the competitor's site site using search engine optimization
  * Getting crazy and promising top rankings in 7 days
  * Kick off new blog website on Friday, October 19th, 2007; in the early AM
  * Fire up Google Analytics on 10/20/07
  * Get link in post from friend's blog 10/20/07; comment on other blogs with related topics
  * Joined Technorati 10/21/07
  * Signed up for Blogger and MySpace on 10/22/07
_**Results - Good News!**___• Indexed by Google on October 21st, 2007, and
again on 10/25/07 under existing domain• Excellent ranking using competitors
keywords and name of organization and domain in 2-3 days; using new
keywords._**Followup on 11/10/07:**_

  * Number one (#1) ranking in Google above competitor
  * About 41 pages cached (28 posts and no comments)
  * Increasing membership
  * Client now in discussions with competitor about the original objective.
_WordPress Plugins in use for this site_

  * Google Sitemaps
  * All in One SEO Pack
  * Google Sitemaps
  * obsocialbookmarker
  * WordPress Mobile Edition
  * WP Navigation Tool (NAVT)
  * podPress
